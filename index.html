Brilliant International School (BIS), established in 2013, is an inclusive school offering Creche, Pre-school, Primary, and Junior High education, with NGO support for underprivileged children.

Learning opportunities for every child

We provide neurotypical and atypical teaching experience, with a stimulating academic curriculum, matched to their abilities, in a clean, serene, caring, learning environment combined with a unique international focus.

Creche

A safe & nurturing environment for toddlers to play & learn.

Pre-School

Pre-school focuses on early childhood education and development.

Primary

Primary School provides a solid foundation for kids 6-12 years

Unearthing Unique Potential

Our goal is that every child should be aided and equipped, to achieve independence and self-sufficiency to bring out the best potential in students.
